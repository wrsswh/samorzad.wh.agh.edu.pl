About us

Faculty Council of the Student Council (abbreviated as WRSS WH) is part of the University Council of the Student Government of the AGH University of Science and Technology.

We are the only organization authorized to represent the student community of the Faculty of Humanities at AGH.

You can become a member of the student government by submitting your candidacy in the general elections organized by the Faculty Student Electoral Commission of the Faculty of Humanities.

WRSS WH offers both action-based and permanent volunteering, which can be used to fulfill student internships at the Faculty of Humanities at AGH.
