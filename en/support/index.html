Support

We are a group of students at the Faculty of Humanities (WH) who have resolved many crises. We regularly engage in conversations with faculty, administration, and the authorities of both the Faculty and the University.

If you need support or advice, feel free to contact the appropriate person or unit at AGH. However, if you feel uncertain or unsure of your situation, consult with WRSS.
