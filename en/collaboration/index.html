Our Flagship Projects#

Plant Day#

Plant Day 2024 is a pilot event offering the AGH Faculty of Humanities community the opportunity to learn about the benefits of growing plants at home. We are open to bartering and sponsorships related to promoting environmental protection and sustainable consumption practices.

Faculty Parties at Klub Studio#

We organize faculty parties in collaboration with other Faculty Student Councils and the Academica Foundation. We are open to hosting themed events and sponsored parties.

Welcome Pack for First-Year Students#

We prepare environmentally sustainable welcome packs for first-year students to help them settle into a new city and promote responsible, healthy living in Kraków. We welcome the inclusion of promotional materials and discount codes tailored to our students.

For more projects and collaborations, check out the Projects section.
